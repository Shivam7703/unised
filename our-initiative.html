Who we are

UNISED (Unit of Science and Educational Development), under the mentorship of Department of Computer Science and Engineering of IIT Kanpur – an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India under the Institutes of Technology Act, is working towards improving the quality of education in Govt. Schools across India. It is an ISO 9001:2015 certified society, registered at NITI Ayog & MoE for implementation of projects promoting Innovation in Education like RAA (Rastriya Avishkar Abhiyan), ICT (Information communication Technology), CAL (Computer Aided Learning), Atal Tinkering Lab (ATL), Innovative Smart Classes. Focus of our intervention is to create an innovative and integrated environment in schools through continuous training, mentoring & support supervision for building the capacity of teachers and encouraging children to think, explore, tinker and develop scientific temperament.
